## Key points
- EHACare charges tenants: $1/patient/year
- BetterCare cost to EHA: ~€0.25/patient/year (400K slots pre-purchased = ~€100K/year)
- Margin exists but billing gap (no native EMR billing) is an active sales blocker
- Odoo ERP handles billing and HMO claims — not the EMR
