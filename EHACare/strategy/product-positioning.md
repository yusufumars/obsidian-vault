# Product Positioning — EHACare

**Status**: Current  
**Source**: [Confluence](https://ehealthnigeria.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/EHACARE/pages/169377804)  
**Last updated**: 2026-04-12

## Key points
- Positioned as the configurable clinical OS for African health networks
- Primary target: multi-site private health groups who outgrow single-clinic EMRs
- Differentiation over Helium Health: workflow engine, openEHR standards, multi-tenancy architecture
- Active sales objection to address: no native billing (Odoo integration is the workaround)
