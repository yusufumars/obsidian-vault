# SWOT Analysis — EHACare

**Status**: Current  
**Source**: [Confluence](https://ehealthnigeria.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/EHACARE/pages/251691018)  
**Last updated**: 2026-04-12

## Strengths
- Configurable workflow engine (unique in Nigerian market)
- OpenEHR standards (data portability, interoperability)
- Multi-tenancy architecture

## Weaknesses
- No native billing (active sales objection)
- Pre-PMF — no proven repeatable sales motion yet
- BetterCare dependency (vendor risk)

## Opportunities
- First-mover with external clients (Hope Children Hospital)
- Clinical AI platform (Databricks + Claude)
- Growing private healthcare market in Nigeria

## Threats
- Helium Health market dominance
- BetterCare pricing changes
- Regulatory uncertainty (NDPR, healthcare data)
