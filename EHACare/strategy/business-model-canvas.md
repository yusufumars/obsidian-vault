## Key points
- Revenue stream: $1/patient/year per tenant
- Key cost: BetterCare licensing (~€100K/year for 400K patient slots)
- Key partners: BetterCare (platform), Odoo (billing/ERP)
- Value prop: configurable workflow engine + openEHR data standards + multi-tenancy
